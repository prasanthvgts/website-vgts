About us

Accurate intra-operative measurements and performance tracking to
empower surgeons for exceptional patient outcomes.

Curium.life is a dedicated team of surgeons and deep-tech scientists
committed to advancing the safety, precision, and outcomes of
abdominal and minimal access surgery.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower surgeons with advanced tools that enhance surgical precision, streamline workflows, and improve patient outcomes. Through our innovative solutions, we aim to redefine the standards of care in surgical practice, focusing particularly on complex abdominal wall reconstruction, HPB (hepato-pancreato-biliary) surgery, colorectal surgery, and gynecological & pelvic surgery.

Our Mission

We envision a future where surgical procedures are significantly safer, and patient outcomes are optimized through the integration of AI and deep learning into every step of the surgical process. Our vision is rooted in the belief that technology, when harnessed correctly, can vastly improve the lives of both patients and healthcare providers.

At Curium.life, we harness the power of AI to transform surgery, providing real-time insights and personalized guidance to support surgeons in critical moments. Our technology enhances surgical precision by analyzing detailed imaging, helping surgeons navigate unexpected challenges and tailoring care to each patient's unique anatomy. By bridging skill gaps, reducing the risk of human error, and ensuring meticulous planning, our AI acts as a second set of eyes, making surgeries safer and more efficient. Beyond the operating room, our system continues to monitor patients, speeding up recovery and reducing complications. We are dedicated to making advanced surgical care accessible globally, empowering surgeons to deliver the best possible outcomes for their patients.
