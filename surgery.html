Precision Surgery:
Elevating Outcomes with SurgiMeasure

At Curium, we believe that accurate measurements are not just a technical necessity but the key to achieving exceptional surgical results. By integrating advanced measurement tools, we empower surgeons to perform with unparalleled precision, making informed decisions and executing deliberate actions.

Every step in surgery demands precision, and precision starts with accurate measurements. Whether it's determining mesh size or assessing bowel length in bariatric procedures, precise measurements are crucial for enhancing surgical performance and ensuring safer, more reliable outcomes.

Experience Next-Level Precision with SurgiMeasure

Introducing SurgiMeasure: Your cutting-edge solution for real-time surgical measurements. Designed to seamlessly integrate into the operating room, SurgiMeasure provides:

  • Real-Time Measurements: Effortlessly measure distances between the tool tip and anatomical landmarks, directly overlaying data onto your surgical video display.
  • Voice-Controlled Operation: Enjoy hands-free convenience with voice commands, minimizing workflow disruption and enhancing efficiency.
  • Customizable Units: Choose between millimeters (mm) and centimeters (cm) to fit your specific measurement needs.
  • Seamless Integration: Connects effortlessly with existing monitors and recording systems, ensuring a smooth and uninterrupted workflow.
  • Advanced AI Systems: Automatically detects and tracks the tip of the surgical instrument, adapting to variations in camera position and magnification with precise calibration.
  • Anatomical Landmark Recognition: Recognizes pre-defined anatomical points and allows for selection via voice commands.
  • 3D Reconstruction: Creates a virtual model for accurate volume measurements, taking surgical precision to the next level.

SurgiMeasure is engineered to provide reliable and immediate feedback, seamlessly blending into your surgical environment without requiring extensive changes to current protocols. Embrace the future of surgery, where precision is not just a goal but a reality.
